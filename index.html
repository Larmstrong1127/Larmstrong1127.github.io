About Me

Building things that matter

I'm a software engineer and game developer based in Issaquah, WA. I hold both a Master's and Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Saint Martin's University, where I earned Dean's List recognition every year from 2019 to 2024.

I've built an end-to-end Unity horror game (Echoed Nights) with full ownership — from design and AI architecture through final QA. I've also built and shipped DentaVision — a live, full-stack AI SaaS integrating Claude Vision to parse dental treatment plans into patient-facing visit schedules, drawing directly on my experience as a Patient Care Coordinator. I've also delivered multiple ASP.NET Core and React applications across different domains.

I care about clean, purposeful code and getting projects across the finish line. Outside of engineering, I competed in Track and Field at Saint Martin's for five years — the same discipline I bring to every codebase. I'm actively seeking an entry-level software engineering role where I can contribute across the full stack and keep building things that actually ship.
